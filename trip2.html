Best mom ever, hottest bum on the island—Joel’s mission is to grab it at every romantic break. 💕

🏨 Our Home Base

Condado Vanderbilt Hotel — beachfront elegance with pools for the boys, a stellar spa for us, and sunset views that make Joel whistle at that bum.

📅 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Sunset Stroll & Welcome Dinner

Land, drop bags, then wander Condado Beach hand-in-hand. Dinner at Oceano to toast our week of family fun and sneaky romance.

Day 2 — Old San Juan Colors

Explore rainbow-bright streets, snack stops at Señor Paleta, and cannon selfies at Castillo San Felipe del Morro. Wyatt & Knox: epic hide-and-seek among 500-year-old walls.

Day 3 — Romantic Daytime #1: Cloud Spa Escape

For us: Couples massage + champagne at Cloud Spa.
For the boys: Supervised kayak eco-tour on Condado Lagoon via Kayaking Puerto Rico. They’ll brag about manatee sightings while we brag about… other things. 😉

Day 4 — Rainforest Quest

Hike waterfalls in El Yunque National Forest, then feast on frituras at the legendary Luquillo Kiosks. Mud on shoes, smiles on faces.

Day 5 — Romantic Evening #1: Sea-View Fine Dining

Dinner for two: Ocean-front table at La Perla. Candlelight, waves, Joel’s hand on that bum, life is good.
Boys’ night: Room-service pizza, movies, and a new Switch game challenge.

Day 6 — Art & Soccer Mash-up

Morning inspiration at Museo de Arte de Puerto Rico. Afternoon beach soccer tournament (Wyatt’s footwork vs. Knox’s goalie dives) while we cheer and cuddle on the sand.

Day 7 — Romantic Daytime #2 + Farewell Café

Parents: Quick rooftop dip & mimosas, sneaking a final bum-grab before packing.
Kids: Chill at hotel kids’ club making souvenir crafts.
Final coffee & mallorca at Café Don Ruiz, then off we fly—bags full of keepsakes, hearts full of family love.

✨ Bonus Romantic Evening #2: City-Lights Night Walk

Post-dinner stroll along Paseo de la Princesa—just us, string lights, and cheeky squeezes while the boys crash back at the hotel.

Joel • Sara • Wyatt (13) • Knox (11) — Puerto Rico 2025 🏝️

Puerto Rico Awaits, Sara 🏝️

This one’s filled with soul and sunshine. You picked Puerto Rico — where warm beaches, colorful streets, rainforest waterfalls, and unforgettable food are waiting for you, Joel, Wyatt, and Knox. And yes, there’s quiet time built in too.

🏨 Where We’ll Stay

Condado Vanderbilt Hotel – Elegant, family-friendly, right on the beach in the heart of San Juan. Pools, spa, walkable boardwalks, and all-day views.

📅 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & Oceanfront Sunset

We land, settle in, and walk the ocean boardwalk with a stop at Oceano for an early dinner. Watch the palm trees move as the sun sinks behind the Caribbean.

Day 2 – Explore Old San Juan

Wander cobblestone streets, see colorful Spanish buildings, and tour the epic Castillo San Felipe del Morro. Grab treats from Señor Paleta and lunch at Princesa Gastrobar.

Day 3 – Romantic Spa Morning 🌿

While Wyatt and Knox enjoy a guided kids-only kayak and wildlife tour near the Condado Lagoon, Sara and Joel spend a quiet morning at Cloud Spa. Meet back for lunch at Café Carli.

Day 4 – Rainforest Waterfall Adventure

We head out to El Yunque National Forest for a hike through tropical canopies and a dip in a natural waterfall. Stop at the Luquillo Kiosks on the way back for local fried snacks and fresh juice.

Day 5 – Romantic Dinner by the Bay 🌅

Joel and Sara sneak off for a dinner at La Perla, one of the most romantic restaurants in the Caribbean. The boys are just across the marina at a supervised game lounge inside the hotel.

Day 6 – Chill Day: Art, Soccer & the Sea

Morning at Museo de Arte de Puerto Rico for family culture + anime sketch exhibits. Kids play beach soccer in the afternoon while parents walk the Condado shore.

Day 7 – Coffee & Departure

Brunch at Café Don Ruiz, one last walk through Old San Juan, and off we go — heart full, soul full.

🍽️ Must-Try Puerto Rican Eats

  • Mofongo – smashed plantain + garlic + meat
  • Empanadillas & Alcapurrias – best from Luquillo kiosks
  • Arroz con Gandules – PR-style rice and beans
  • Tembleque – coconut pudding dessert

The Big Apple Awaits, Sara 🗽

You picked New York City — where the lights never dim and every block brings something unforgettable. This week is a mix of action, wonder, and unexpected magic for our whole family.

🏨 Where We’ll Stay

Residence Inn Times Square – Spacious suite for the four of us, right near the heart of Broadway and Bryant Park. Breakfast included. Easy access to subways and everything.

📅 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & Times Square Lights

Touch down, settle in, then head straight into Times Square for the lights, street performers, and a giant family selfie. Dinner at Carmine’s for classic Italian sharing plates.

Day 2 – Central Park + Nintendo NY

Stroll through Central Park: playgrounds, rocks, and rowboats. Then head to Nintendo NY in Rockefeller Center for gaming fun. Lunch nearby at Junior’s.

Day 3 – Romantic Morning in the Sky ✨

While Wyatt and Knox enjoy a supervised immersive VR & laser tag session at SPYSCAPE, Joel and Sara escape to the Edge NYC for skyline views and a Champagne brunch.

Day 4 – Statue of Liberty & 9/11 Memorial

Morning ferry to the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island. Afternoon visit to the 9/11 Memorial Museum. Dinner at Eataly Downtown.

Day 5 – Romantic Broadway Night 🎭

The boys are treated to pizza, movies, and game time at the hotel while Joel and Sara walk to dinner at The Lambs Club, followed by a Broadway show (Your choice, Sara 😉).

Day 6 – Brooklyn Bridge & Street Art

Walk the Brooklyn Bridge, explore DUMBO’s art walls, and try food trucks at Time Out Market. Afternoon at the American Museum of Natural History.

Day 7 – Bryant Park Breakfast & Departure

Bagels and coffee in Bryant Park. Chess, carousel, and people-watching before heading home with stories from the city that never sleeps.

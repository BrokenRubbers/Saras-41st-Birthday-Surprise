🏝️ Sara’s Caribbean Surprise

Dear Sara — this Puerto Rican escape was chosen with you in mind. Rich in culture, flavor, and romance, this week is for you, Joel, and our adventurous sons Wyatt and Knox to experience something unforgettable together.

Day 1 – San Juan Arrival 🌇

  • Check in at the luxurious Condado Vanderbilt Hotel
  • Evening walk along the boardwalk with dinner at Oceano

Day 2 – Old San Juan Exploration 🏰

  • Tour El Morro Fortress and pastel streets
  • Lunch at Princesa Gastrobar
  • Kite flying with Wyatt and Knox by the sea wall

Day 3 – Rainforest & Rooftop Romance 🌳

  • Morning hike in El Yunque Rainforest
  • Romantic Evening #1: Joel takes Sara to rooftop dinner at Elena
  • Wyatt & Knox enjoy a movie and snacks with a sitter at the suite

Day 4 – Museums & Spa 🖼️🧖

  • Joel and Sara sneak off to Well & Being Spa for a couple’s treatment
  • Meanwhile, Wyatt and Knox get creative fun at Museo del Niño

Day 5 – Island Vibes & Cruise 🌊

  • Chill day at Playita del Condado beach
  • Romantic Evening #2: Catamaran sunset cruise, champagne and stars
  • Kids: Domino’s + retro games night in the suite

Day 6 – Culebra Day Trip 🐢

  • Ferry to Culebra
  • Snorkel, swim, and spot sea turtles with the boys
  • Taco lunch at Zaco’s and shaved ice on the ferry back

Day 7 – Café & Farewell 🇵🇷☕

  • Final breakfast at Café Don Ruiz
  • Walk along Paseo Caribe and fly home with full hearts

Peace in Paradise, Sara 🐢

You’ve chosen Siesta Key—a place of warm water, perfect sand, and slow mornings. This week is built to recharge with joy, laughter, and a few quiet hours just for the two of us.

🏨 Where We’ll Stay

Tropical Breeze Resort – Right near the beach and Siesta Key Village. Spacious, walkable, private pool access and a truly local feel.

📅 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & Siesta Sunset

Settle in and walk to Siesta Key Beach for soft sand, calm water, and our first sunset dinner at The Cottage.

Day 2 – Mote Aquarium & Village Stroll

Explore sharks, manatees, and sea turtles at Mote Marine Laboratory. Afternoon games and treats in Siesta Key Village. Family dinner at Siesta Key Oyster Bar.

Day 3 – Romantic Morning Paddle & Spa 🌺

Joel and Sara kayak through the Lido Key mangrove tunnels and get beachside massages at The OM Shoppe & Spa, while Wyatt and Knox enjoy a supervised nature scavenger hunt & camp at Marie Selby Gardens.

Day 4 – Myakka Wildlife Safari

Drive out to Myakka River State Park for gator-spotting, canopy walks, and boat tours. Dinner at Sharky’s on the Pier.

Day 5 – Romantic Evening in St. Armands 🌙

While the boys watch anime and play video games with a sitter at the hotel, Sara and Joel have a night out in St. Armands Circle—shopping, cocktails, and fine dining at Indigenous.

Day 6 – Beach Games & Village Treats

Soccer and frisbee on the beach. Afternoon at FoxLeaf Donuts and handmade gifts in Siesta Village. Family dinner at The Hub Baja Grill.

Day 7 – Last Swim & Farewell

Wake up for sunrise on the beach, breakfast at Toasted Mango Café, and head home full of salt air and memories.

🍽️ Must-Try Local Eats

  • The Cottage – creative island-inspired plates
  • Indigenous – intimate, seasonal, upscale dining
  • Sharky’s on the Pier – oceanfront seafood
  • Toasted Mango – best breakfast around
  • FoxLeaf Donuts – anime-worthy sweets

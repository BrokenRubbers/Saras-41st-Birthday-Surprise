Aloha, Sara 🌺

Surprise! You’ve picked Hawaii—one of the most breathtaking, soul-refreshing places on Earth. Joel planned this adventure so you and the boys could experience something unforgettable together—and sneak in a little romance too.

🏨 Where We’ll Stay

Hyatt Place Waikiki Beach – Just steps from the ocean with breakfast included, a rooftop pool, and walkable access to everything in Waikiki.

📅 7‑Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & Sunset Stroll

We land, check in, and walk along Waikiki Beach as the sun sets. Dinner at Duke’s Waikiki for live music⁠—laid-back island vibes.

Day 2 – Surf, Zoo & Beach

Wyatt and Knox start their morning with a beginner surf lesson together! Then we’ll head over to the Honolulu Zoo, followed by a relaxed lunch at Barefoot Beach Cafe in Kapi‘olani Park.

Day 3 – Romantic Spa Day 💆‍♀️

While the boys enjoy a supervised afternoon at Lucky Strike Social arcade, Joel and Sara recharge at Na Ho‘ola Spa with lunch by the water.

Day 4 – North Shore Adventure

We’ll drive up to the North Shore to check out the Dole Plantation, grab garlic shrimp at Giovanni’s Shrimp Truck, and unwind at Turtle Beach.

Day 5 – Sunset Dinner Cruise 🌅

Joel and Sara enjoy a romantic sunset dinner cruise. Meanwhile, Wyatt and Knox stay at the hotel for pizza and a Nintendo tournament.

Day 6 – Lagoon Paddle & Shopping

Morning beach sports for the boys; you two paddleboard in the lagoon and enjoy iced coffee. Then we’ll shop together at Ala Moana Center.

Day 7 – Pool, Musubi & Fly Home

One last dip, breakfast at Musubi Cafe Iyasume, then we fly home sun-kissed and smiling.

🍽️ Must-Try Food Spots

  • Duke’s Waikiki – beachside staples
  • Marukame Udon – create-your-own noodle bowls
  • Leonard’s Bakery – golden malasadas
  • Giovanni’s Shrimp Truck – North Shore classic
  • Señor Paleta – refreshing popsicles

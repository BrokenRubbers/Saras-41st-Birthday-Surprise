🏨 Our Home: Hyatt Place Waikiki Beach

📅 Our Week in Paradise

Day 1: Welcome to Waikiki

We'll check in, then stroll Waikiki Beach and enjoy a sunset dinner at Duke’s Waikiki, right on the sand.

Day 2: Surf's Up & Honolulu Zoo

Wyatt and Knox catch waves with beginner surf lessons while we watch. Then we'll head to the Honolulu Zoo.

Day 3: Romantic Spa & Arcade Day

Sara and Joel enjoy massages at Na Ho‘ola Spa. Meanwhile, the boys have supervised fun at Lucky Strike Arcade.

Day 4: North Shore Adventure

Explore Dole Plantation, enjoy lunch at Giovanni’s Shrimp Truck, and unwind at Turtle Beach.

Day 5: Sunset Dinner Cruise 🌅

Joel and Sara cruise along the coast at sunset, enjoying dinner and romantic views, while the boys enjoy pizza and Nintendo back at the hotel.

Day 6: Lagoon Paddle & Ala Moana Shopping

Morning paddleboarding in the lagoon, then family shopping at Ala Moana Center.

Day 7: Farewell Breakfast & Homeward Bound

A quick swim, breakfast at Musubi Cafe Iyasume, and we say aloha to Hawaii—until next time.

🏨 Where We’ll Stay

Hyatt Place Waikiki Beach – Just steps from the ocean with breakfast included, a rooftop pool, and walkable access to everything in Waikiki.

📅 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & Sunset Stroll

We land, check in, and walk along Waikiki Beach as the sun sets. We'll grab dinner at Duke’s for live music and laid-back island vibes.

Day 2 – Surf, Zoo & Beach

Wyatt and Knox will take a beginner surf lesson together! Then we’ll visit the Honolulu Zoo and relax at Kapi‘olani Park. Casual lunch at Barefoot Beach Cafe.

Day 3 – Romantic Spa Day 💆‍♀️

While the boys are off to a supervised afternoon arcade and game session at Lucky Strike Social, Joel and Sara will enjoy a quiet spa morning at Na Ho‘ola Spa and lunch by the water.

Day 4 – North Shore Adventure

We’ll drive up to the North Shore to explore Dole Plantation, grab garlic shrimp at Giovanni’s Shrimp Truck, and relax at Turtle Beach.

Day 5 – Romantic Dinner Cruise 🌅

Evening romance alert: Joel and Sara take a sunset dinner cruise off the coast. The boys will stay at the hotel with pizza and a Switch tournament.

Day 6 – Paddleboard & Island Chill

The boys enjoy a beach sports morning while you both paddleboard on the lagoon and grab iced coffee in Waikiki. Afternoon shopping at Ala Moana Center.

Day 7 – Morning Swim & Fly Home

One last swim. Local breakfast at Musubi Cafe Iyasume. Then we fly home, sun-kissed and smiling.

Gulf Shores is Calling, Sara 🐬

You picked a quiet stretch of sugar-white sand, warm Gulf waters, and plenty of space to relax as a family. Joel knew this would be a peaceful retreat that lets us slow down and connect—without sacrificing fun.

🏨 Where We’ll Stay

The Beach Club Resort & Spa – Private beach, kid-friendly pools, spa for you and me, and great food—all walkable within the resort.

📅 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Beach Arrival & Bonfire Dinner

Check in, hit the beach, and enjoy a private family bonfire with marshmallows and a seafood boil at sunset.

Day 2 – Dolphin Cruise & Wharf Fun

Morning dolphin cruise with the boys, then rides and arcade time at The Wharf. Dinner at LuLu’s.

Day 3 – Romantic Spa Escape 💆‍♂️

While Wyatt and Knox enjoy a fun afternoon at the supervised kids’ adventure zone, we’ll slip away to the Beach Club spa for massages and cocktails on the boardwalk.

Day 4 – Gulf State Park Exploration

Hike or rent bikes to explore Gulf State Park. We’ll stop for a picnic and wildlife watching along the back bays and trails.

Day 5 – Romantic Dinner at Voyagers 🌅

Evening for us at Voyagers, a waterfront fine dining gem. The boys will stay back with their favorite pizza and games, supervised in the condo.

Day 6 – Mini Golf, Arcade & Beach Games

Fun day: mini golf at Tiki’s Mini Golf, soccer on the beach, and an afternoon milkshake at The Yard.

Day 7 – Last Swim & Fly Home

Final dip in the pool, one last walk down the beach, and then we head home happy and salty.

🍽️ Must-Try Coastal Eats

  • LuLu’s – kid-perfect with beach games & live music
  • Voyagers – fine dining with sunset views
  • Original Oyster House – old-school Southern seafood
  • Sunliner Diner – 1950s vibes + epic milkshakes

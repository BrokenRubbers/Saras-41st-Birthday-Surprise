Welcome to Miami, Sara 🏖️

You’ve picked a week of warm ocean air, Latin rhythm, family fun, and a bit of luxury. Joel planned this Miami escape to be packed with flavor, fun, and just enough sparkle to make it unforgettable.

🏨 Where We’ll Stay

Loews Miami Beach Hotel – Right on the beach, family-friendly, pool with cabanas, and within walking distance to Ocean Drive, Lincoln Road, and art deco sights.

📅 7-Day Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & South Beach Sunset

Settle in and stroll the South Beach boardwalk at golden hour. Dinner at El Dorado for rooftop tacos and skyline views.

Day 2 – Miami Children’s Museum & Bayside

Start with the Miami Children's Museum — interactive tech, sports stations, and creativity labs. Lunch and boat spotting at Bayside Marketplace.

Day 3 – Romantic Morning & Arcade Fun 💕

Sara and Joel will sneak away for a private brunch and couples massage at The Betsy’s Wellness Garden, while Wyatt and Knox spend the morning at Miami Arcade Center (fully supervised).

Day 4 – Jungle Island Adventure

Family fun at Jungle Island: zip lines, wildlife encounters, and botanical paths. Dinner at Monty's Coconut Grove on the water.

Day 5 – Romantic Night in Little Havana 🎶

The boys get dinner + movies at the hotel while you two slip away to Ball & Chain for Cuban food and live music in Little Havana.

Day 6 – Beach Games & Outdoor Art

Morning beach soccer and paddleball, then head to Wynwood Walls to explore graffiti art and murals. Lunch at the artsy Wynwood Kitchen & Bar.

Day 7 – Pool, Brunch & Home

One last poolside morning with local brunch from Big Pink, then pack up and fly home glowing.

🍽️ Must-Try Miami Eats

  • Versailles – iconic Cuban food
  • Big Pink – over-the-top brunch spot
  • Joe’s Stone Crab – famous seafood tradition
  • Ball & Chain – mojitos and live salsa
  • Azucar Ice Cream – wild Cuban flavors
